Lasagna Recipie

Description

The best food to eat when you're starving, The sauce and the cheese make for a spectactular combination. This recipe is passed on from generation to generation and is one that every person falls in love with.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Ricota Cheese
  • Meat Sauce
  • Mozzerlla Cheese

Steps

  1. Pre Heat Oven to about 350F-375F(around 190C)
  2. Layer the ingredients
    1. Place 3-4 Lasagna Noodles over Sauce
    2. Spread 1/3 of the riccotta cheese mixture over the noodles
    3. Top with 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese and 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese
    4. Repeat the layers (sauce, noodles, ricotta, mozzarella, Parmesan) two more times.
    5. End with a final layer of noodles, remaining sauce, and top with the remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  3. Cover and Bake: Cover the lasagna with aluminum foil (to prevent sticking, you can spray the foil with cooking spray to avoid the cheese sticking to it). Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
  4. Uncover and Finish Baking: Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  5. Rest Before Serving: Let the lasagna stand for about 15 minutes before serving to allow it to set.
